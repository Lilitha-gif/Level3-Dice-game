Dice Game

Game Rules

  • Roll the dice to generate a random number.
  • If you roll a one, the turn switches to the other player.
  • If you roll a number other than one, add it to your current score.
  • If your current score plus total score is 100 or more, you win!
  • Hold your score to add your current score to your total score.
  • First player to reach 100 wins the game!
  • Press reset to start a new game, with all scores set to 0 and Player 1 starting.
